Art and Design in the Digital Age: How Technology is Transforming Degrees and Industry Practice
Site Author
 Basit Zain


Abstract
 In this site, Zain delves into the topic of how technology has greatly impacted the domains of art and design, both in terms of how these subjects are taught and how they are used in the industry. He goes into depth on the development of technology and people's opinions. Effects on Instructional Strategies and Curriculum Technology use have significantly changed the methods of instruction for degrees in art and design. Utilizing digital tools and software has given experts in the field new job options and given students a broader understanding of how technology can be used to create and modify art and design aspects.

 Art and Technology: How Technology is Changing the Art World
Site Author
 Pinot's Palette Naperville
Abstract
 This site also talks about the impact of technology. However, it focuses more on the art industry than the design industry.  Technology is altering the art industry by providing audiences with new methods to interact and experience art, as well as new opportunities for creativity and invention. Technology is changing how artists create and engage with their audiences in the art world. The site, then, gives readers examples of the type of impact technology has on the art world. There are about four different categories with explanations and reasonings of how technology plays an important role in the art industry.

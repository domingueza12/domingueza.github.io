Brief History of Digital Art
Site Author
 WORLD ART NEWS


Abstract
 This site contains information on how digital art came to be. The timeline starts from 1960s to the 21st century.  It shows and explains examples of digital art and how it came to be. When artists started experimenting with computers as a medium for creating visual art in the 1960s, that is when the earliest kinds of digital art emerged. The possibilities for creative expression were substantially increased by digital art, which led to the emergence of new artistic forms and styles.The site gives more examples and detailed explanations for the reader to understand the long process of the development.

 The digital revolution
Site Author
 Philip B. Meggs
Abstract
 This site gives a detailed explanation of the revolution of digital design unlike the first link.  Designers and artists were able to use computer graphics intuitively due to tool icons that could be controlled by a mouse or graphic tablet. Individual designers were able to use typesetting tools thanks to digital computers, which sparked an experimenting phase in the creation of new and unique typefaces and page layouts. This also mentions how technology plays an important role in web design. Designing for navigation on the website and the use of hypertext links to access more content are only two of the many new factors that come with web design.
